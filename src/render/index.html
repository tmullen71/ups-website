About Me

I received my PhD from the University of Groningen, my MPhil from Trinity College, Dublin, and my BA from the University of Washington.

For 10 years I taught computer science at Tsuda College in Tokyo. Previous to that I spent two years as a postdoctoral researcher at the Japan National Institute of Informatics.

I'm interested in languages and language technology, interactive media, games, and computer graphics.
